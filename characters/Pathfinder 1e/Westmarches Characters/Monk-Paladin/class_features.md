# ?'s Class Features
## Monk
### Draconic Might
- All Wis-based class abilities use Cha instead

### AC Bonus
- When unarmored and unencumbered, add [Cha] bonus to AC and CMD
- Gain +0 to AC and CMD
- Applies against touch attacks and when flat-footed
- Lose when immobilized or helpless, armored, carrying a med/hvy load, or carrying a shield

### Bonus Feat
- At lvl 1, 2, and every +4 after that, gain a bonus feat from following list:
    - Catch Off-Guard
    - Combat Reflexes
    - Deflect Arrows
    - Dodge
    - Dragon Style
    - Improved Grapple
    - Intimidating Prowess
    - Scorpion Style
    - Throw Anything

### Flurry of Blows
- Can make a Flurry of Blows as a full-attack action, making an additional attack at full BAB
- This stacks with extra attackes from *haste* etc
- Can make these attacks with any combo of unarmed strikes and monk quality weapons
- No penalties and no additional attacks for multiple weapons

### Stunning Fist
- Gain Stunning Fist as a bonus feat
- Can choose to apply fatigued instead of stunned

### Unarmed Strike
- Gain Improved Unarmed Strike as a bonus feat
- Can make unarmed strikes with fists, elbows, knees, and feet, meaning that they can be made with hands full
- No such thing as an off-hand for striking unarmed
- Full Str bonus on unarmed strikes, deal lethal damage, can choose nonlethal with no penalty
- Treated as both manufactured and natural weapon for effects that improve/enhance them
- Damage: 1d6

### Evasion
- If succeed on Ref save vs attack that would do half dmg, take no dmg instead
- Only usable in light or no armor, and while not helpless

### Fast Movement
- Gain enhancement bonus to land speed: +10ft
- Only when not in armor or carrying med/hvy load

### Ki Pool
- Ki points: 1/2 lvl + [Cha] mod, replenished by 8hrs rest/meditation
- Ki strike: spend 1 ki point as a swift action to make one extra attack at full BAB during flurry of blows
    - Unarmed attacks count as magic for overcoming DR

## Paladin
### Lay on Hands (Su)
- Heal a target or self by touch for 1d6 hp, standard action for others, swift for self
- Need one free hand to use
- May use against undead to deal 1d6 dmg with melee touch atk, non-provoking, no save
- Per day: 1/2 paladin lvl + Cha mod
- May use it on another creature as move action, but this does not impart any mercy, only hp
- May sacrifice increments of 5hp as part of using lay on hands to add this amt to healing provided

### Detect Evil (Sp)
- Can use *Detect Evil* at will
- As move action, can concentrate on a single item or individual within 60ft
    - Determines if evil, learn strength of aura as if having studied for 3 rds

### Aura of Good (Ex)
- Detects as Faint (if not otherwise stronger) to *Detect Good*

### Rightful Retribution (Su)
- Swift action, or free if using lay on hands only on self, may affect next creature that benefits from lay on hands with aura of heavenly fire
- Lasts Cha mod rounds, opponents that succeed at melee atks against target take 1d6 fire dmg, plus 1/2 Cha mod divine bonus dmg (divine bonus not subject to energy resistance or DR)
- Divine bonus dmg doubled against evil creatures and negated against good creatures
