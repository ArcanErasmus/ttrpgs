# ?'s Class Features
## Warpriest
### Spellcasting (Divine)
- Prepared 6-level caster, Cleric list (spells of 7 or higher lvl not considered on list)
    - Prepare from entire cleric list
#### Spontaneous Casting
- Can expend any prepared spell to cast a "cure" spell of the same or lower lvl

#### Orisons
- Not consumed when cast

### Aura (Ex)
- Has a Good aura as a cleric

### Blessings (Su)
- Can select two blessings from deity's domains
- Gain each blessing's minor power at lvl 1, major power at lvl 10
- Can use blessings in total (3 + 1/2 warpriest lvl + Wis mod)/day, Wis-based DC
- If also cleric lvls, blessings must match chosen domains
- Chosen: Healing, Protection

### Focus Weapon
- Gain Weapon Focus as bonus feat for a weapon of your choice

### Sacred Weapon (Su)
- Applies to deity's favored weapon (light mace and heavy mace), and any Weapon Focus choices
- Can choose to use base damage instead based on level
    - 1-4: 1d6
    - 5-9: 1d8
    - 10-14: 1d10
    - 15-19: 2d6
    - 20: 2d8
- At lvl 4, can enhance one of your sacred weapons as swift action, total of (warpriest lvl) rds/day
    - Give weapon +1 enhancement bonus (+1 per 4 lvls above 4)
    - Can enhance additional wpns (or other end of double weapon) on further rds, each individually consume rounds
    - Can give special abilities in exchange for lower bonus, wpn must already have at least +1 bonus
    - Abilities (+1): *defending*, *flaming*, *frost*, *ghost touch*, *keen*, *shock*
- Bonus and abilities chosen on first use each day, cannot be changed until following day
- Do not apply if others wield the weapon
- Can end as free action at start of turn

### Bonus Languages
- Bonus languages include Abyssal, Celestial, Infernal

### Fervor (Su)
- Can use (1/2 warpriest lvl + Wis mod)/day
- Swift action to fast-cast a prepared spell
- Heal for 1d6 (+1d6 per 3 lvls above 2), standard on others, swift on self
- Can use to harm undead, no save

### Bonus Feats
- Bonus combat feat at every 3rd lvl
- Treat warpriest lvl as BAB or fighter lvl for prereqs

### Channel Energy (Su)
- Standard action, expends 2 uses of fervor, non-provoking
- Must present a holy symbol to use
- Choose to either heal living or harm undead, does fervor dice amount
- 30 ft radius burst, choose to include self or not
- Undead can Will save (Wis-based) for half dmg
