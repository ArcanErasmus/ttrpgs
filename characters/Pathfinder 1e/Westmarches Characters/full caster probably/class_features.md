# ?'s Class Features
## Arcanist
### Spell Casting
- Hybrid prepared/spontaneous 9-level caster, Sor/wiz list
    - Prepare from spellbook, num per day affected by "spells known" modifiers
    - Cast spontaneously from prepared list, num per day affected by Int
    - Can apply metamagic spontaneously as sorcerer, or prepared as wizard (cannot mix)

#### Spellbooks
- Prepare from spellbook
- Begin knowing all lvl 0 sor/wiz, and 3+Int mod lvl 1
- Gain 2 of any castable when lvl up
- Can learn from other wizard/arcanist spellbooks

#### Cantrips
- Don't take spell slot

### Arcane Reservoir (Su)
- 3+lvl points cap, regain 3+(lvl/2) daily on preparing spells
- Spend 1 point as free action to increase spell's DC or CL by +1

### Consume Spells (Su)
- Can expend a spell slot as a move action to regain arcane points equal to its level
- Max Cha mod times per day

### Arcanist Exploits
- Gain exploit at lvl 1 and every +2 after

#### Familiar (Su) [lvl 1]
- Gain a familiar as a wizard of arcanist lvl

#### Dimensional Slide (Su) [Feat bonus, lvl 1]
- As part of a move or withdraw action, spend 1 arcane point to add non-provoking teleport
- Can be at any time during the move, counts as 5ft of movement
- Move up to 10ft/lvl to any location in LoS

#### Counterspell (Su) [lvl 3]
- May spend arcane point to attempt to counterspell
- Requires spending a spell slot (at +1 lvl of target unless target is currently prepared)
- Acts as *dispel magic* counterspelling, but does not trigger "on counterspell success" effects
